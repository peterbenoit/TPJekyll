Digital First

Digital First is recognizing that more than half of visitors to CDC.gov come on mobile devices first.

Digital First thinking can improve your web and social media efforts and provide other benefits, like helping you free content and engaging images from PDFs and elevate them to your web pages (HTML) using CDC’s responsive design web templates, motion graphics, and video.
